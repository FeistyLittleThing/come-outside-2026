Where to lay your head

Sleep under the stars
(or not)

★ ★ ★

The venue has a few ways to spend the night.

Camping: There are so many places to camp we could never fill them. Rooms and trailers are limited and may go fast. Plenty of places if you have a camper van or car camping setup.

Vintage Trailers: Be forewarned that as of last year the vintage trailers were VERY rustic. Picture the project trailer that you never got around to. No heat, no air conditioning, some battery powered lights (probably). Each one does have a bed. I hear they have clean sheets, but bringing your own bedding or sleeping bag is a good idea. Drink enough and you probably won't care. Bonus, you don't have to set up a tent so more time for fun. You get the point, don't ask for further details.

Rooms: There are only a handful of rooms. They're nice. I think most of them share a bathroom, but it's not a big area so it's close.

Directions & logistics

Finding Dundee Lodge

★ ★ ★

Dundee Lodge is located in the Oregon wine country hills, about 30 miles southwest of Portland.

Address: 50845 SW Dundee Rd, Gaston OR 97119

From Portland: Head southwest on Hwy 99W through Newberg and Dundee, then follow signs toward Gaston. About 45 minutes from Portland depending on traffic. Plenty of parking on site for whatever you're driving.
