Directions & logistics

Finding Dundee Lodge

★ ★ ★

Dundee Lodge is located in the Oregon wine country hills, about 30 miles southwest of Portland.

Address: 50845 SW Dundee Rd, Gaston OR 97119

From Portland: Head southwest on Hwy 99W through Newberg and Dundee, then follow signs toward Gaston. About 45 minutes from Portland depending on traffic. Plenty of parking on site for whatever you're driving.
